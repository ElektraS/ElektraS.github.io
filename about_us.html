Gravitiie


Gravitiie is a serious game developed by three web enthousiasts : Aurelie, Leslie and Oumaima; students from ENSIIE (Strasbourg, France).

Try out this challenging and entertaining journey across the Solar System !

While playing, you will learn some of the spatial mecanics and gravitation phenomena. You may also check out our ‘Information’ section where you will find interesting facts about the planets of our system.

As the international Space Apps Challenge is a two-day hackathon, we present you a beta version of the game. In the first level, you should send the satellite into orbit around Venus.

Good luck !
